Now Accepting:

SNAP, HIP, WIC, and Senior Coupons Accepted.

We're pleased to accept your SNAP/EBT card. Please visit our Information booth located at Fortini Gelato to swipe your card to receive tokens that may be used at any of our vendors' stands. A new program called Healthy Incentive Program (HIP) will run at the market this summer. All SNAP customers with receive additional funds for purchasing fruit and vegetables at the market. Twin Oaks Farm, Intervale Farm, and Roundhill Orchards accept HIP. Apply for food stamps through the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP). Find out if you qualify for food stamps at www.gettingfoodstamps.org.
